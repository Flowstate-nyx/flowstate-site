// CASE STUDY 01 — BOUTIQUE RESORT
Sababa Resort
San Pedro La Laguna · Lake Atitlán · Guatemala

The Challenge

A 25-room boutique resort with an on-site restaurant, running 100% on cash across two Square POS accounts. Hotel rooms lived in Cloudbeds. Finances lived in heads and paper. The owner had no unified view of revenue, no way to connect reception to restaurant sales, and no automations. Every daily close was manual. Every reconciliation was a guess.

What We Built

  • Square POS integration across Reception + Restaurant accounts
  • Cloudbeds PMS sync for all 25 rooms
  • 5 automated workflows: sales sync, cancellation tracking, daily summaries, stock alerts, close-of-day
  • Unified dashboard pulling both POS streams into one view
  • 16 profit optimization moves identified in audit
